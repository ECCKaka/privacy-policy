Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 07, 2025

Privacy-First Design

VoceAI is designed with privacy as a core principle. All audio recordings and transcriptions are processed and stored locally on your device. Audio files and transcripts are never transmitted to our servers or any third parties. The app functions entirely offline and does not require an internet connection.

Data We Do NOT Collect

VoceAI is designed to protect your privacy. We do not collect, transmit, or store:

  • Audio recordings or voice data
  • Transcription text or content
  • Personal identifying information
  • Usage analytics or statistics
  • Device identifiers or IP addresses

How Your Data is Stored

All data created within VoceAI remains on your device:

  • Audio recordings are stored locally in the app's secure storage area on your iPhone or iPad
  • Transcriptions are processed on-device using local AI models and stored locally
  • You have complete control over your data and can delete recordings and transcripts at any time within the app
  • If you delete the app, all associated data is removed from your device

Third-Party Services

VoceAI does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data collection services. The app functions completely independently without external connections.

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with anyone because we do not collect or have access to your data. All audio and transcriptions remain private on your device.

Children's Privacy

Our Service does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because all processing happens locally on the device, no data is transmitted or stored by us.

Security of Your Personal Data

Your audio recordings and transcriptions are protected by your device's built-in security features, including encryption and device passcode/biometric authentication. Since data never leaves your device, it is not subject to network transmission risks.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • All data is stored locally on your device under your control
  • You can delete any recordings or transcripts at any time within the app
  • You can export your transcripts to share as you choose
  • Uninstalling the app removes all associated data from your device
